About Me

I’m an experienced Data and Business Analyst with over five years of cross-functional experience spanning data analysis, civil engineering, and quality assurance. I specialize in transforming complex, messy data into clear, actionable insights through statistical analysis, KPI reporting, and data-driven problem solving, using tools such as SQL, Python, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau.
Throughout my career, I’ve worked in quality- and performance-focused roles at Outplex and Data Vimenca, where I improved data accuracy, monitored operational KPIs, and coached teams to meet performance and compliance goals. I’ve also strengthened my communication and project coordination skills in client-facing roles at SiriusXM and MPower, aligning analytical outputs with business objectives and stakeholder needs.
I’m certified in Google Data Analytics and Google Project Management, bringing a structured, process-oriented approach to analytics, project planning, and continuous improvement. With a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering and fluency in English, Spanish, and French, I combine technical rigor, analytical thinking, and strong communication to deliver insights that drive smarter decisions and measurable impact.
